Terms of Service — Sumo Squared Arena

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Sumo Squared Arena is a personal, single-account automated content publishing tool. It records short gameplay clips of a physics-based game and posts them to video platforms (including TikTok) using each platform's official API on behalf of the account owner.

What this service does

  • Runs on a local schedule, once per day.
  • Generates one short video clip.
  • Uploads that clip to the operator's own connected accounts via each platform's official upload API.

What this service does not do

  • Does not operate on behalf of any third party or accept other users.
  • Does not collect, sell, or share data from platform end users (viewers).
  • Does not use platform data for any purpose beyond publishing the operator's own content.

Acceptable use

This tool is used solely to publish original content created by the operator. It complies with each connected platform's developer terms and content policies.
